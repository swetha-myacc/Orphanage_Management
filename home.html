Who we Are?

Vision

As orphanages are increasing day by day, Helping Hands aims to reach out to the needy orphans by providing volunteering opportunities. Our platform allows users to search and find orphanages on the internet and make donations according to their preferences.

Mission

Our mission is to support orphanages and children in need by connecting them with donors and volunteers. We strive to make a positive impact on the lives of these children by providing them with essential resources and opportunities for a better future.

Who we Are?

We are a dedicated team committed to making a difference in the lives of orphaned children. Through our platform, we aim to raise awareness about orphanages and facilitate contributions from individuals and organizations to support their cause.
